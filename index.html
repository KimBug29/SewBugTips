    Fabric PreCuts


    Precuts samplers are bundles of coordinating quilting fabrics, cut to standardized sizes, and sold as a single unit.
    They are easy to use and also a great way to sample an entire fabric collection. PreCut names can be confusing, check out some examples with their definition.

    • Honey Buns

      1.5" x Width of Fabric (WOF)

    • Jelly Rolls

      2.5" x Width of Fabric (WOF)

    • Mini Charm Packs

      2.5" x 2.5" per piece

    • Charm Packs

      5" x 5" per piece

    • Layer Cakes

      10" x 10" per piece

    • Fat Quarters

      18" x 22" per piece

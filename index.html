Fabric PreCuts


Precuts samplers are bundles of coordinating quilting fabrics, cut to standardized sizes, and sold as a single unit. They are easy to use and also a great way to sample an entire fabric collection. PreCut names may not sound like fabric cuts, but they do sound delicious! From honey buns and jelly rolls to layer cakes and fat quarters (there is some irony with the last name in the list), browse some images with names and definitions.

Honey Buns

1.5" x Width of Fabric (WOF)
